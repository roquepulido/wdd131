Site Name

ScriptureStream

A flowing, organized way to study scriptures

Site Purpose

Helping Latter-day Saints study smarter, track their scripture journey, and retain doctrines—one verse at a time.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

What can this web app offer?

  1. Reading Tracker
    • Log daily/weekly scripture reading (customizable books/chapters).
    • Visual progress bars, streaks, and stats (e.g., "You've read 80% of the Book of Mormon!").
    • Optional goal-setting (e.g., "Finish the New Testament in 3 months").
  2. Scripture Flashcards
    • Pre-loaded decks (e.g., "Christ's Parables," "Topical Guide Themes").
    • Create custom flashcards (add personal notes/verse references).

Scenario 2

Who is the main target audience user?

  • Active LDS Members
  • Youth & Young Adults (Ages 12-30)
  • New Converts & Returning Members
